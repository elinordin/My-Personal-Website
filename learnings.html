Learnings from this brief

  • Never use var, use let or const.
  • Use IDs on sections so that they can be referenced and linked to in your navbar.
  • Canva is great, easy to use, get inspiration or color palettes from and you can adjust the colors in the element/photos to match.
  • Try mobile first web design in the next project, easier to make it responsive.
  • Write down your learnings so that you realise you're making progress.
  • To explain something to someone else is a great way to put something in words and to simplify it to yourself.
  • Comments are awsome!
  • Make a list of must-haves and nice-to-haves to keep you focusing on the right things.
  • For the next project, don't use pure black or white on your page, it's best practice.
  • if (stuck) {take break}!
